Joe Bray's Top Games

Video games are to me, as they are to many others, a big part of my life.
They've taught me patience, given me a passion to solve puzzles, and, most of all, spurred in me a competitive drive that pushes me to be the very best.
These are just a few of the games that I felt have truly impacted my life in the greatest ways.
